Rock Paper Scissors Rules


Objective

  • Rock Paper Scissors is a luck-based, randomized game. The version here is a single player version that allows the user to play against a computer! The Objective is to choose a character that "beats" the computer's chosen characters, such as rock crushing scissors, or paper covering rock, and more!

Game Setup

  • This game page specifically has 3 buttons vertically laid, that allow you to choose your character.
  • Once your character is chosen, you may select the "SHOOT!!!" button, which tells the computer to choose a character, and display the winner.
  • Once the "SHOOT!!!" button is pressed, the computer will also display your choice, alongside the computer's choice.

Winning

  • The game ends when the "SHOOT!!!" button is pressed.
  • In the unlikely case that the computer and player choose the same character, the computer will display "It's a Tie!"
