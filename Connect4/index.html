Connect 4 Rules


Objective

  • Connect Four is a two-player game where the goal is to be the first to form a line of four of your colored discs, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.

Game Setup

  • The game is played on a 7x6 grid.
  • Players choose a color (traditionally red or yellow).
  • Players take turns dropping one of their colored discs into any unoccupied column from the top.

Turns

  • Players alternate turns, dropping one disc at a time into the grid.
  • Discs are placed in the lowest available space within the chosen column.

Winning

  • The game ends when one player successfully forms a line of four of their colored discs.
  • The line can be horizontal, vertical, or diagonal.
  • If the grid fills up without a winner, the game ends in a draw.
