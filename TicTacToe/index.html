TicTacToe Rules

Objective

  • TicTacToe is a 2 player game, with a "Player vs. Computer" and "Player vs. Player" option. The first player to get their mark 3 in a row horizontally, vertically, or diagonally wins!

Game Setup

  • The game is played on a 3x3 grid.
  • The first player to move is ALWAYS X, and the second to move is always O. When doing "Player vs. Computer," the player will always be X and move first.

Turns

  • Players go back and forth picking a space to mark each turn.
  • If the board fills up with no spaces remaining, and neither player has won, the game ends in a draw.

Have fun!
